About Me

Designing with intention.

As a Taiwanese - Aboriginal born, Canadian designer, I aim to portray a unique world of imagination, built upon my multi-cultural experience and expertise. I hope to leave an everlasting impact among individuals and society through interdisciplinary design, whether it be a newfound emotion, thought, or self-identity.

Aside from dedicating my time to design, I immerse myself with various activities for relaxation and explore areas of life. Sports and music has accompanied me for the majority of my life. As a recreational boxer and violinist / pianist, I allow myself to reach into a separate world of immersive physical experience that strengthens my imagination and creativity on a deeper level.
